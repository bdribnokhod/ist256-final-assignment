At Firehouse Cafe, we proudly brew more than just great coffee — we honor service, sacrifice, and community. Founded by a team of first responders, our cafe is a tribute to the heroes behind the badge, the uniform, and the call to serve. Blending the rustic charm of a firehouse with the warm comfort of a neighborhood cafe, we’ve created a space where everyone — responder and civilian alike — is part of the family. Every cup we pour is a small thank-you to those who dedicate their lives to protecting and caring for others. If you have any questions, please send us a message.

About our cafe

Founded in the 1960s in Newmanstown, Pennsylvania, Firehouse Cafe was built by a group of local firefighters, police officers, and medics who wanted a place to recharge after long shifts and celebrate the spirit of service. Set in a converted old firehouse, the cafe quickly became a second home where first responders and neighbors could come together over a hot cup of coffee and shared stories. Today, Firehouse Cafe stands as a proud tribute to the men and women who dedicate their lives to protecting others, honoring the legacy of courage, compassion, and community in every cup we serve.
